What is Motion.com?

Motion.com is physics motion experiments web application, which can help students to understand about Linear, Projectile, and Simple Harmonic Motion. In our website, there are many features to support the person who interested in physics. First, the simulation which is written by JavaScript will help you more understanding about physics motion. The second is we have physics news for people who interested in them. Moreover, we are going to develop our new features, and we will update them later.
